Me Button: Kirby

~~~

For the "Me" Button, we decided to use Kirby as the character we would be focusing on.
We chose him due to his popularity in the gaming world and his cute aesthetic.
Kirby is well known for eating, enjoying life, and adventuring to defeat bad guys!

To begin the project we each thought of 10 different ideas and sketched them
My ideas were:

  • Infinite Kirby Feeding via circular belt
  • Click to feed
  • Mash button to unleash Kirby's Power!
  • Upgrade Kirby graphics through the ages
  • Draw Kirby
  • Catch Falling Food with Kirby!
  • Kirby x Mario Game
  • DDR Kirby
  • Kirby ASMR
  • Kirby Kombos

10x10 Method: Refined Sketches

~~~

Here are my refined sketches for the top 3 sketches that were chosen.

Kirby Meme Generator Refined Ideas:

  • Click Kirby to change Meme on TV
  • Kirby swallos M Tomato and becomes the Meme
  • Click on Kirby for him to transform to a random Meme

Kirby Copy Abilities:

  • Click and drag Kirby to a copy ability, then click for him to inhale which transforms him!
  • Kirby starts off in a copy ability. Double click to attack in his copy ability. Use the scroll wheel to change copy ability forms
  • Click on the screen and Kirby will start flying to where you clicked. If Kirby hits a copy ability, he transforms. Double click Kirby to use the copy ability move tot speed him up to where you clicked next

Cooking Kirby with Responses:

  • Kirby inhales icons depicting other characters and transforms
  • Click and drag Kirby to the Copy Abilities or Characters side. A roulette shows up and you click Kirby again to aim for the middle icon. Kirby inhales and transforms accordingly
  • Scroll click on Kirby to change graphics after he has transformed!
  • Once Kirby has transformed, double click to see him perform his transformed ability!

Implementation

~~~

After looking over our refined sketches together we combined our ideas to create our button.
Here are the ideas we came up with when comparing our 30 refined sketches:

  • Chef Kirby would be the button
  • Beside Chef Kirby would be a giant Mirror to show off Memes
  • There would be a walking Kirby
  • Clicking Chef Kirby once produces a Copy Ability for Kirby to consume
  • Clicking Chef Kirby twice produces a M Tomato and when consumed produces a Meme
  • Clicking and holding down on Chef Kirby produces food for Kirby to consume
  • When consuming a Copy Ability, Kirby would transform and the background would change
  • Everytime Kirby sucks in a sound is made
  • When consuming a M Tomato or food Kirby would transform back to normal
  • Kirby makes one of several "poyo" sounds when eating
