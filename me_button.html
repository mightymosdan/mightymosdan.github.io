Me Button: Kirby

~~~

The Kirby Button is a web based app all about Kirby! A floating chef Kirby is interactable via mouse clicks. You can click, double-click, and click and hold to produce different things for our walking Kirby to inhale! Keep an eye on the magical mirror when Kirby eats a M Tomato! The Kirby button exemplifies Kirby's powerful Copy Abilities, his ability to produce laughter and joy via Memes, and his insatiable hunger!

Character Focus: Kirby

~~~

For the "Me" Button, we decided to use Kirby as the character we would be focusing on.
We chose him due to his popularity in the gaming world and his cute aesthetic.
Kirby is well known for eating, enjoying life, and adventuring to defeat bad guys!

Top 3 out of Top 10

~~~

Our team then decided to choose the top 3 ideas from our top 10...

The 3 ideas that come from our top 10 sketches are:


  1. Kirby Meme Generator
  2. Kirby Copy Abilities
  3. Cooking Kirby with Responses
We chose to filter our top 10 to top 3 because we were confident that we would be able to utilize our other good ideas into these 3 main ideas.

We decided not to move forward with certain ideas that would require multiple buttons as that would take away from the goal of focusing on the user experience of a button(singular)

As mentioned previously, we also decided not to move forward with turning this button into a game as the focus should be on the character and the technicalities of gamifying the button would be too complex.

Implementation

~~~

After looking over our refined sketches together we combined our ideas to create our button.

Button Ideas:

  • Chef Kirby would be the button
  • Beside Chef Kirby would be a giant Mirror to show off Memes
  • There would be a walking Kirby - this Kirby gets to inhale!
  • Every button click makes a sound
  • Clicking Chef Kirby once produces a Copy Ability for Kirby to consume
  • Clicking Chef Kirby twice produces a M Tomato and when consumed produces a Meme
  • Clicking and holding down on Chef Kirby produces food for Kirby to consume
  • When consuming a Copy Ability, Kirby would transform and the background would change
  • Everytime Kirby sucks in a sound is made
  • When consuming a M Tomato or food Kirby would transform back to normal
  • Kirby makes one of several "poyo" sounds when eating
  • A title screen that shows you how to interact
  • Background music with an accompanying toggle on/off
  • Sound effects when the item hits the floor
  • Chef Kirby and Magic Mirror are floating in the sky as Kirby walks
