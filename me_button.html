Me Button: Kirby

~~~

For the "Me" Button, we decided to use Kirby as the character we would be focusing on.
We chose him due to his popularity in the gaming world and his cute aesthetic.
Kirby is well known for eating, enjoying life, and adventuring to defeat bad guys!

To begin the project we each thought of 10 different ideas and sketched them
My ideas were:

  • Infinite Kirby Feeding via circular belt
  • Click to feed
  • Mash button to unleash Kirby's Power!
  • Upgrade Kirby graphics through the ages
  • Draw Kirby
  • Catch Falling Food with Kirby!
  • Kirby x Mario Game
  • DDR Kirby
  • Kirby ASMR
  • Kirby Kombos

Implementation

~~~

After looking over our refined sketches together we combined our ideas to create our button.
Here are the ideas we came up with when comparing our 30 refined sketches:

  • Chef Kirby would be the button
  • Beside Chef Kirby would be a giant Mirror to show off Memes
  • There would be a walking Kirby
  • Clicking Chef Kirby once produces a Copy Ability for Kirby to consume
  • Clicking Chef Kirby twice produces a M Tomato and when consumed produces a Meme
  • Clicking and holding down on Chef Kirby produces food for Kirby to consume
  • When consuming a Copy Ability, Kirby would transform and the background would change
  • Everytime Kirby sucks in a sound is made
  • When consuming a M Tomato or food Kirby would transform back to normal
  • Kirby makes one of several "poyo" sounds when eating
